About Us

Our Story

Soaperb products (Pty) Ltd is the brainchild of Salih Mobara, a university student whose endeavour was to create a cheaper alternative to everyday soaps and detergents for use in commercial, industrial and domestic settings. After months of research and consultations with professionals in the field of chemical production, Soaperb products was born.

History

Established in February 2015, we started as a home based business supplying to a limited clientele base. We have since grown from our humble beginnings and are now operating from a factory setting. We have become the preferred supplier for various companies in the catering, hospitality and retail industries.

Our mission

Our primary mission is to build lasting relationships with clients while supplying them with high quality products at the cheapest possible price. All our products are manufactured in-house at our factory, as such, we have complete control over the quality of our products. Our aim is to become the leading supplier of soaps and detergents in the industry starting with the Western Cape and growing from there on.
